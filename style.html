Type Faces

Primary: Open Sans

This is the main typeface. The following styles can be used:

  • Regular 400
  • Regular Italic 400i
  • Semi-Bold 600
  • Semi-Bold Italic 600i
  • Bold 700
  • Bold Italic 700i

Secondary: Oswald

This typeface is used for Headings. The following styles can be used:

  • Light 300
  • Regular 400
  • Medium 500
  • Semi-Bold 600
  • Bold 700

Mixed Modular Type Scale: 1.25, 1.5, and 0.75
